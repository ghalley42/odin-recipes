Mac and Cheese

Description

Delicious and gooey baked mac and cheese!

Cheese sauce and pasta come together in a dish more scrumptios than even Henry Cavill!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Cavatappi
  • 8 oz Shredded Aged Cheddar Cheese
  • 4 oz Shredded Gruyere Cheese
  • 4 oz Shredded Gouda Cheese
  • 3 Cups Milk
  • 6 Tbsp Butter
  • 3 Tbsp Flour
  • Splash of Hot Sauce
  • 1 tsp Mustard Powder
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • Half a Nut Fresh Ground Nutmeg
  • 2 Cloves Fresh Garlic
  • 1 cup Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta to just before al-dente following the instructions on the package.
  2. Drain your pasta and set aside in a 9" x 13" baking dish.
  3. Melt 3 Tbsp of your butter over medium heat in a small sauce pan until it is bubbling.
  4. Add your flour and whisk for 2-3 minutes until a smooth roux has formed.
  5. Add your mustard powder and whisk to combine.
  6. Slowly add your milk while whisking to combine the milk and roux until smooth.
  7. Whisk your milk and roux mixture over medium heat until it begins to thicken.
  8. Whisk in your hot sauce, garlic powder, and nutmeg.
  9. Add salt and ground black pepper to taste.
  10. Turn off the heat and fold in all but 4 oz of your shredded cheese one handful at a time until the sauce is smooth.
  11. Fold the sauce into the pasta in the baking dish.
  12. Melt the remaining butter over medium head until it starts to brown.
  13. Sautee your garlic and breadcrumbs until they are brown and crispy.
  14. Turn off the heat and mix in the parsley
  15. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs and remaining shredded cheese over the top of your pasta and sauce.
  16. Cover the baking dish with tinfoil and bake at 350 F for 25-35 minutes.
  17. Remove the tinfoil and crisp the topping under the broiler.
  18. Let it cool for a bit and then absolutely monster the shit out of it. It is delicious (bussin). Don't hold back.
